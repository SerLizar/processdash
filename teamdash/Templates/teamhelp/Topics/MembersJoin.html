Join Team Project (Team Members)

Each team member must join the team project.

  1. Before you can join the project, you must have successfully installed the Process Dashboard. Start the Process Dashboard and leave it running.
  2. Your team leader will send you a special URL that you can use to join the team project. Open that URL in a Web browser.

    If you get an error message like "This page cannot be displayed" when you attempt to visit this page, contact your team leader and ask him or her to start the Team Dashboard, then click the refresh button on your Web browser. If your team leader tells you that the Team Dashboard is running and you still cannot view the page, see the Troubleshooting Network Connectivity help topic.

  3. The Team Project Setup Wizard will walk you through the steps involved in joining the team project. Read each page carefully and supply the information requested.
  4. When the wizard finishes, you can close the browser window.
    Note: Under certain unusual circumstances, you may see a warning like the one below:

    The dashboard was unable to add your personal earned value schedule to the team earned value schedule. Please tell your team leader about this problem; they will be able to add your schedule to the team schedule manually.

    If you see this message, make certain to tell your team leader. Make certain to tell your team leader the name you gave your earned value schedule.
