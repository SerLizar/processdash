I am writing to you on behalf of the members of the Software Process Dashboard Initiative. As ardent supporters of the Personal Software Process(SM), we have developed an open-source support tool that automates many of the process activities described in the book A Discipline for Software Engineering, written by Watts Humphrey. To enhance the support this tool provides to engineers, we would like permission to include the following copyrighted PSP(SM) materials in our tool:

These materials would be integrated into the tool so users could receive process guidance and support at the click of a button.

We would distribute the resulting tool, free of charge, over the Internet. The tool would be distributed primarily via our website, http://processdash.sourceforge.net/, as a free download. Occasionally, we would also like to distribute the software on CDs for the convenience of our users.

Because our tool has been explicitly developed in an open-source model, it would be available to users free of charge. Our goal from the beginning has been to promote the use of the Personal Software Process; we feel that a zero-cost support tool would remove one of the most common hurdles engineer face. By encouraging the use of the PSP, we feel that this tool would enhance sales of the book A Discipline for Software Engineering.

We look forward to your response.
