Tailoring the Process Dashboard for Your Organization

Every organization is different, and high-maturity teams have differing needs from their metrics support tools. The Process Dashboard strives to meet these needs by offering a high degree of customizability to support different processes.

Still, not every organization needs extensive customizability. Many organizations, for example, would be content to use the standardized metrics collection framework and process forms historically described by the Team Software Process (SM). Unfortunately, TSP (SM) licensing does not permit the distribution of such materials as open source, so the open source Process Dashboard distribution cannot include them.

In the future, if TSP materials can be licensed for distribution with the Process Dashboard, such materials would be made available at http://www.tuma-solutions.com/

In the meantime, high-maturity organizations using the Process Dashboard should plan for and set aside time to perform the process customization steps (listed in the sections below) before they need to use the dashboard in support of a particular team project.
