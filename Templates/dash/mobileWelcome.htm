Process Dashboard Mobile Companion App

A mobile/smartphone application is available for use with the Process Dashboard.

The mobile app works alongside the Process Dashboard, making it possible to perform a number of common tasks from an iPhone®, iPad®, or AndroidTM device:

  • Reviewing the list of tasks you're responsible for
  • Logging task time, and editing past time logs
  • Updating the estimated time for a task
  • Marking tasks complete

The data you collect in the app is automatically synchronized with data in the Process Dashboard, and vice-versa.

The mobile application does not replace the Process Dashboard. You will still need to open the Process Dashboard program on your computer to export your data for team rollups, and to perform other project related tasks. Still, the app can be useful for collecting project data when you are away from your computer.
