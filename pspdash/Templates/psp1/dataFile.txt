= Estimated LOC

Estimated Base LOC=0.0
Estimated Deleted LOC=0.0
Estimated Modified LOC=0.0
Estimated Base Additions LOC==![(+	~Base Additions/\d+/LOC)]
Estimated New Objects LOC==![(+	~New Objects/\d+/LOC)]
Estimated Reused LOC==![(+	~Reused Objects/\d+/LOC)]
Estimated Object LOC==![(+	Estimated Base Additions LOC	Estimated New Objects LOC	Estimated Modified LOC)]
Estimated Added LOC==![(-	Estimated New & Changed LOC	Estimated Modified LOC)]
Estimated New Reused LOC==![(+	~New Objects/\d+/{New Reused?}LOC)]
Estimated Total LOC==![(-	[(+	Estimated New & Changed LOC	Estimated Base LOC	Estimated Reused LOC)]	Estimated Deleted LOC	Estimated Modified LOC)]
Estimated Max LOC=0.0
Estimated Min LOC=0.0
Estimated New & Changed KLOC==![(/	Estimated New & Changed LOC	1000)]
Estimated New & Changed LOC/Beta0=0.0
Estimated New & Changed LOC/Beta1=0.0
Estimated New & Changed LOC/Range=0.0
Estimated Total LOC/Range==![(#=	Estimated New & Changed LOC/Range)]
Estimated New & Changed LOC/Interval Percent=0.0
Estimated New & Changed LOC/Probe Method="D
Estimated New & Changed LOC/R Squared=0.0

= Actual LOC

Base Additions LOC==![(+	~Base Additions/\d+/Actual LOC)]
New Objects LOC==![(+	~New Objects/\d+/Actual LOC)]
Reused LOC==![(+	~Reused Objects/\d+/Actual LOC)]
Object LOC==![(+	Base Additions LOC	New Objects LOC	Modified LOC)]
New Reused LOC==![(+	~New Objects/\d+/Actual {New Reused?}LOC)]
New & Changed KLOC==![(/	New & Changed LOC	1000)]



==
== Data Elements to calculate overall process metrics.
==

Estimated LOC per Hour==![(*	60	[(/	Estimated New & Changed LOC	Estimated Time)])]
LOC per Hour==![(*	60	[(/	New & Changed LOC	Time)])]
Defects per KLOC==![(/	Defects Injected	New & Changed KLOC)]
